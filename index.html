About Me

I am Mahyar Salamati, a highly motivated individual with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, achieving an outstanding grade of A+. With diverse experiences, including leading the NEON Advertising Team and serving as an Educational Advisor at Kamal High School, I have developed strong leadership and guidance skills. Additionally, as an IT Supervisor at Pars Arian Zamin Geological Research Center, I excel in managing IT operations and producing content. Throughout my career, I have been recognized for my achievements, including a patent for an Intelligent Plant Growth and Nutrition System and a Silver Award in the International Young Scientist Innovation Exhibition. My computing skills encompass various applications, such as Microsoft Office Suite, Adobe Creative Suite, and programming languages like C, C++, Java, HTML, and Python. I possess a versatile skill set, including expertise in bioinformatics, biotechnology, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and deep learning, as well as social network analysis, desktop publishing, and educational counseling. With a passion for learning and a keen eye for detail, I am driven to contribute to the fields of technology and education.
